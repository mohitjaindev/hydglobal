Students

We develop potential

“Potential” means that you have or are currently earning a bachelor’s or master’s degree in computer science or information technology. “Potential” means that you have a skillset that can be applied to real-world, project-driven training.

Opportunity

We are always looking for students and recent graduates for internships and full-time options. We are open to hearing from anyone with the above qualifications who wishes to build their professional reputation under the guidance of highly skilled, experienced developers.

We Develop People

Under our expert supervision, students and recent grads learn what it takes to become a professional consultant for HYR Global Source through project-driven training, which means:

You gain hands-on software development and consulting experience from current projects.
You upgrade your skills and learn which ones are in demand.
Because IT is changing so quickly, we teach you how to stay ahead of the learning curve.
We advise you on your career path.
You learn from failure with trusted advisors internally, before you enter the workplace.
You build a professional reputation as a consultant who possesses skills for the future.

We Develop trust

Our software development and IT consultants and our clients are our greatest assets. We treat our professionals how we would like them to treat our customers. The most highly qualified knowledge and experience will not help our company succeed unless we value our business relationships. Therefore, we recognize the importance of our people by taking care of them with the best benefits, mentorship and opportunities for upward mobility.

If you are interested in benefiting your career through a position with us.
